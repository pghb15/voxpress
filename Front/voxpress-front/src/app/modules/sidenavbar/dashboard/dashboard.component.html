Voxpress

Application for Voicemail Management in Amazon Connect
Voxpress integrates with Voicemail Express, a feature designed to provide basic voicemail functionality to Amazon Connect. You easily manage all the voicemails your customers leave. You can listen to them, archive the ones you've handled, or assign them to someone on your team for follow-up. It's a handy spot to keep track of all your customer voice messages and ensure nothing slips through the cracks.
